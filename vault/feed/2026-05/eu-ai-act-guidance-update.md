# Implementation guidance clarifies high-risk AI obligations

Regulators published implementation guidance clarifying documentation and human-
oversight expectations for high-risk AI systems under the EU AI Act.

## What this means

Clients building in regulated EU domains now have clearer targets for technical
documentation and oversight. For active engagements, revisit your high-risk
classification and confirm the delivery plan budgets time for conformity steps.
There's no radar status here — this is a compliance signal: route affected use cases
through the Compliance Tracker and Governance modules.
