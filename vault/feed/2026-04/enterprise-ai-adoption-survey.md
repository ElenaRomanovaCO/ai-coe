# Survey: most enterprise AI pilots stall before production

An industry survey reports that a majority of enterprise AI pilots fail to reach
production, citing data readiness, unclear value, and governance gaps as the top
blockers.

## What this means

This validates the CoE's core thesis: the binding constraint is rarely the model — it's
data readiness, value definition, and governance. Lead engagements with the Maturity
Screener and ROI templates to surface the real blocker early, and design the first
increment to be small enough to actually ship. No radar status; this is positioning
evidence for client conversations.
