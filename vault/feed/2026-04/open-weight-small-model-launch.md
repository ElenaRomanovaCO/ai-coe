# Open-weight small model for private deployments

A new open-weight small language model claims strong instruction-following at a size
that runs on modest hardware, aimed at private and on-device deployments.

## What this means

For clients with hard data-residency constraints (public sector, regulated health),
a capable small model that runs in their own environment can unblock use cases that
can't send data to a hosted API. Quality won't match frontier models — scope it to
narrower tasks (classification, extraction, routing). Radar: **assess** for
privacy-constrained engagements.
