# AI Discovery Workshop — Kickoff Template

A half-day agenda to align stakeholders, surface candidate use cases, and set a
realistic first increment. Use at the start of any engagement with a client at
maturity stage 0-1 (exploring).

## Pre-work

- Share a one-page primer on AI maturity stages.
- Ask each attendee to bring two pain points and one aspiration.

## Agenda (4 hours)

1. **Framing (30 min)** — Goals, constraints, and what "good" looks like in 90 days.
2. **Current state (45 min)** — Data, tooling, skills, and prior attempts.
3. **Opportunity mapping (60 min)** — Group pain points into themes; sketch
   candidate use cases on a value/effort grid.
4. **Risk & guardrails (30 min)** — Regulatory exposure, data sensitivity, change
   readiness.
5. **First increment (45 min)** — Pick one use case; define a thin, demoable slice.
6. **Close (30 min)** — Owners, next steps, and the decision cadence.

## Outputs

A ranked use-case shortlist, a chosen first increment, and a named owner per next
step. Feed the shortlist into the Use Case Ideation and Maturity Screener modules.
