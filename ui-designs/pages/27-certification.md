# Page Brief — Certification & Badging

> Inherits `../design-system.md`. Route: `/modules/certification`. Module 20 (AGENT-19),
> Wave 6. Covers FR-069..071.

## Purpose

Track consultant learning paths, certifications, and earned badges — progress + a trophy
case. Motivational, light gamification (tasteful, enterprise-appropriate).

## Content & data

- **Certifications/paths:** available certs (name, topic, requirements, progress).
- **Badges:** earned + available badges (icon, name, criteria, earned date).
- **Recommendations:** next cert/learning step.

## Primary actions

Browse certs/paths · view requirements · track progress · view earned badges · start a
path · see recommended next step.

## States

Overview (my badges + progress) · cert detail (requirements/progress) · all-badges ·
empty.

## Layout

Top: my earned **badges** (trophy case) + overall progress. Below: available
certifications as cards (with progress rings) and a "recommended next" nudge.

## Design prompt seed

> Design a "Certification & Badging" page for an enterprise AI-consulting platform. Top: a
> "trophy case" of earned badges (distinct but tasteful badge icons with names and earned
> dates) plus an overall progress summary. Below: available certifications as cards — each
> with a name (e.g. "AI Governance Fundamentals", "RAG Solution Design"), a topic, a
> progress ring, and requirements; plus a "recommended next" nudge. Tasteful, professional
> gamification (motivating, not childish); indigo accent + a warm accent for earned badges;
> Inter; rounded-lg; light + dark. Generic certification names — no company names.
