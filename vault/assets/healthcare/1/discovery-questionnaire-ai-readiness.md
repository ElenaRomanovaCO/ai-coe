# Healthcare AI Readiness Questionnaire

A structured questionnaire to gauge readiness before scoping an AI engagement with
a healthcare provider or payer. Answers feed the Maturity Screener.

## Strategy & sponsorship

1. What clinical or operational outcome would justify this investment?
2. Who is the executive sponsor, and what is their risk appetite?

## Data

3. Where does the relevant data live, and how is PHI segregated?
4. What is the state of de-identification tooling and data-sharing agreements?
5. How is data quality monitored today?

## Compliance & governance

6. Which regulations apply (HIPAA, state privacy laws, FDA for device-like uses)?
7. Is there an existing AI or model governance committee?

## Capability

8. What in-house data science and MLOps capability exists?
9. Have prior AI pilots reached production? What blocked those that didn't?

## Change readiness

10. How will frontline clinicians be involved in design and rollout?

Score each area 0-5 and map to the maturity stages to identify the binding
constraint for the first increment.
