# Page Brief — Community & Enablement Hub

> Inherits `../design-system.md`. Route: `/modules/community`. Module 6 (AGENT-07),
> Wave 6. Covers FR-061..063.

## Purpose

Surface community discussions, enablement resources, and peer expertise — "who knows
about X, and where do I learn it?"

## Content & data

- **Discussions:** threads/posts (title, author display name, topic tags, replies).
- **Enablement resources:** curated guides/links by topic.
- **Expertise finder:** find peers by topic/industry experience.

## Primary actions

Browse/search discussions · open a thread · post/reply · find an expert · open a resource.

## States

Hub home (mixed) · discussion list · thread detail · expertise results · empty.

## Layout

A hub home with sections: recent discussions, enablement resources, "find an expert".
Thread detail = a standard discussion view (post + replies).

## Design prompt seed

> Design a "Community & Enablement Hub" page for an enterprise AI-consulting platform. A
> hub home with three sections: "Recent discussions" (thread cards with title, author
> initial-avatar + display name, topic tags, reply count), "Enablement resources" (curated
> guide cards by topic like Bedrock, RAG, Governance), and "Find an expert" (search by
> topic/industry returning peer cards). Include a thread-detail view (original post +
> threaded replies + a reply composer). Friendly-but-professional internal community feel;
> indigo accent; Inter; rounded-lg; light + dark. Use generic display names and topics —
> no company names, no real people.
