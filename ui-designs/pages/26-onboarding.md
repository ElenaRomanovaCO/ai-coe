# Page Brief — Consultant Onboarding Journey

> Inherits `../design-system.md`. Route: `/modules/onboarding`. Module 23 (AGENT-22),
> Wave 6. Covers FR-067..068.

## Purpose

Guide a new consultant through a personalized onboarding path — a sequenced, trackable
"start here" journey through the platform and domain.

## Content & data

- **Journey:** ordered milestones/modules (learn the platform, key concepts, first
  assessment, governance basics, …), each with status (not started / in progress / done).
- **Progress:** overall % + current step.
- **Resources per step:** linked assets/guides.

## Primary actions

Start/resume the journey · complete a step · open a step's resources · skip/customize ·
see progress.

## States

Journey overview (path) · step detail · completed · empty.

## Layout

A vertical/horizontal **stepper** of milestones with progress, a current-step detail
panel (what to do + resources), and an overall progress header.

## Design prompt seed

> Design a "Consultant Onboarding Journey" page for an enterprise AI-consulting platform.
> A guided, trackable path: a stepper of milestones (e.g. "Tour the platform", "AI
> maturity basics", "Run your first assessment", "Governance & compliance 101", "Explore
> the Asset Library") each with a status (done / in-progress / not-started) and linked
> resources; an overall progress header (e.g. "3 of 8 complete"); and a current-step
> detail panel describing what to do with action buttons. Welcoming, motivating-but-
> professional; indigo accent + green completion states; Inter; rounded-lg; light + dark.
> Generic role ("AI delivery consultant") — no company names.
