# Responsible AI Foundations Workshop

A three-hour workshop to establish shared responsible-AI principles before a public
sector organization starts building. Suited to stage 0-1 teams.

## Objectives

- Agree on principles (fairness, transparency, accountability, privacy).
- Map principles to concrete review steps in the delivery process.
- Identify the highest-sensitivity use cases that need extra scrutiny.

## Agenda

1. **Why responsible AI here (20 min)** — Public trust, equity, and legal duties.
2. **Principles to practice (50 min)** — Translate each principle into a checklist
   item owned by a role.
3. **Harms walkthrough (40 min)** — Work a sample use case through a harms
   framework; surface affected groups and mitigations.
4. **Transparency & redress (30 min)** — How decisions are explained and appealed.
5. **Operating model (40 min)** — Who reviews what, and when, in the lifecycle.

## Outputs

A one-page principles charter, a review checklist mapped to delivery stages, and a
shortlist of high-scrutiny use cases. Connect outputs to the Ethics & Bias Checker
and Compliance Tracker modules.
