# FFIEC Expectations for AI in Financial Institutions

FFIEC member agencies apply existing safety-and-soundness and consumer-protection
expectations to AI. Rather than a single AI rule, AI is folded into established
guidance on model risk, third-party risk, and fair lending.

## Key areas

- **Model risk management** — Development, validation, and ongoing monitoring of AI
  models, consistent with longstanding model-risk guidance.
- **Third-party risk** — Due diligence and oversight of AI vendors and model providers.
- **Fair lending / UDAAP** — AI in credit and marketing must not produce discriminatory
  or unfair outcomes; explainability supports adverse-action notices.
- **Information security** — Data used for AI must meet security and privacy controls.

## What this means for engagements

For banking and lending clients, treat AI models as in-scope for model risk management
and third-party oversight from day one. Ensure decisions are explainable enough to
support adverse-action and fair-lending obligations. Pair with the Model Risk Checklist
asset and the Compliance Tracker module.
