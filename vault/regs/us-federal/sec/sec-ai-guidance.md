# SEC and AI in Financial Services

The SEC has signaled scrutiny of how registered firms use and describe AI. Two themes
dominate: accurate disclosure and conflicts of interest.

## Key themes

- **"AI-washing"** — Overstating AI capabilities in disclosures or marketing can draw
  enforcement. Claims about AI use must be accurate and substantiated.
- **Conflicts of interest** — Predictive or recommendation systems that optimize for
  the firm's interest over the client's raise concerns; firms should identify and
  address such conflicts.
- **Recordkeeping** — Communications and decisions involving AI tools remain subject
  to recordkeeping obligations.

## What this means for engagements

For financial-services clients, ensure any external claims about AI match reality, and
review recommendation systems for client-vs-firm conflicts. Keep auditable records of
AI-assisted decisions. Confirm the latest rule status, as guidance in this area is
evolving.
